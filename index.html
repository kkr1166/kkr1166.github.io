Kitra Razin (kee-tra ray-zin) is a composer and composition teacher with a passion for math and data analytics. She has scored over 10 films, written chamber and solo concert music, and produced several original pop songs. She has also worked in consulting, banking, and actuarial science. In June 2020, she graduated with honors from Northwestern University, where she double majored in Music Composition and Mathematical Methods in the Social Sciences.
